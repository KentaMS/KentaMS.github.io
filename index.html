JAPANESE CRAFT BEERS OF THE MONTH: JANUARY

This website is devoted to featuring the finest craft beers I have sampled each month during my year-long journey across Japan. The country is currently witnessing its second craft beer boom since the economic bubble, with dozens of artisanal breweries emerging every year. These breweries all experiment with various beer styles, both old and new, all while using locally sourced ingredients.

MUSCAT HAZY

MALLIKA BREWING

Fresh fruit uppercut. Strong muscat aroma coupled with citrus notes. Sweet yet bitter with an almost viscuous texture. Brewed with 80kg a batch of the local Nagano Shine Muscat and 27g/L of a wide variety of modern aroma hops.

SHIGA KOGEN 1T IPA

TAMAMURA HONTEN

Overwhelming IPA with powerful toffee and toast aromas, along with an explosive yet pleasant bitterness. A beer for the classic IPA enthusiast. The name simply derives from the god forsaken amount of malt used per batch. Best enjoyed in front of a live fire.
